Let's build together!

YVR Builders is a community of tech builders in Vancouver. It's a space for builders to connect, share ideas, collaborate on projects, find users, and gather feedback.

Promotion of products or services will be considered spam unless clearly engaging with the community to gather feedback and not just to sell. Advertisement will not be tolerated, but open discussion of your products is encouraged.

We follow the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct . For enforcement matters, contact qard on Discord.
